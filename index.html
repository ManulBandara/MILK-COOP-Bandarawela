About Our Cooperative

Registered under the Uva Province Cooperative Development Department

The Bandarawela Dairy Producers Cooperative Society Ltd. was established in 1979 with a clear mission: to ensure guaranteed milk prices for dairy farmers while improving their living standards and livelihoods.

Operating from the heart of Uva Province, we serve farming communities across Bandarawela, Ella, Hali Ela, Haputale, and surrounding areas. Our commitment to quality, fair pricing, and community development has made us a trusted partner for hundreds of dairy farmers.

Through continuous product development and modern processing facilities, we produce the trusted 'Ceylon Tasty' brand, delivering fresh, safe, and nutritious dairy products to consumers while maintaining competitive market presence.
